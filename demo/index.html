Demo Instructions

  1. Focus on the time box and enter 6:15 followed by tab; appends 'am'.
  2. Go back and enter 18:15 and hit tab, converts to 6:15pm
  3. Enter 6:15pm and hit tab; nothing changes.
  4. Enter 'elephant' and hit tab; instant validation failure calls custom callback.
